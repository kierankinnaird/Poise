What you get

Everything you need to review client movement data before and between sessions.

Client technical dashboard

Every linked client in one list: latest MQI, last audit date, top technical note. Know where to focus before the session starts.

MQI trends over time

Track each client's Movement Quality Index across audits. See how their technical proficiency changes between sessions.

Technical notes by movement

Drill into any audit and see which technical deviations were detected across 9 movement patterns, each rated by severity. Acknowledge findings to confirm your independent review.

Movement audit invite code

Share one code. Clients enter it in the Poise app and are linked to your dashboard instantly. No admin, no onboarding calls.

Professional use notice: Poise is a technical auditing tool. The data provided -- including the Movement Quality Index (MQI) -- is an estimation based on computer vision and NSCA performance benchmarks. It is not a substitute for your independent clinical judgement. Professionals must verify AI-generated technical notes before incorporating them into a clinical treatment plan. Poise does not provide a clinical diagnosis and is not a regulated medical device.
